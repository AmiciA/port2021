CUHK Business School

2017‚Äì2018 ‚Ä¢ Web design, design system
bschool.cuhk.edu.hk

Project details

Following a radical rebrand earlier in the decade, CUHK's faculty of business needed to apply their new branding in a unified way across its varied programme sites, all while balancing internal communication objectives with inspiring success stories. Before beginning our redesign, I worked with another designer to conduct focus group sessions and one-on-one interviews with students and staff.

Feedback from these sessions informed the rest of the design process and even helped to identify a previously overlooked stakeholder category: non-English speaking parents of undergrads. With wide range of sites designed under the same style, I naturally worked with our developer to create a pattern library that has since informed the development of further sites commissioned by the Business School.
